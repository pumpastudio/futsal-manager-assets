Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

vFutsal Manager ("we", "our", or "us") operates the vFutsal Manager mobile application (the "Game"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Game, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your email address (when creating an account). The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The Game does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Analytics)

2. Authentication Data (Firebase Auth)

When you register or log into vFutsal Manager, we use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your account. This service collects and stores your email address and a unique user identifier (UID) to allow you to log in, save your game progress, and manage your team across devices.

3. Log Data and Crash Reports

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Game, in case of an error or crash, we collect data and information (through Firebase Crashlytics) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Game, the time and date of your use of the Game, and other statistics to help us fix bugs.

4. In-App Purchases and Advertisements

vFutsal Manager does not serve advertisements and does not offer any in-app purchases or microtransactions using real currency.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Account and Data Deletion

We believe in user autonomy and data privacy. You can delete your vFutsal Manager account and all associated data at any time through one of the following methods:

  • In-Game: Navigate to the settings menu inside the Game and select the "Delete Account" option. This will permanently delete your authentication details and game progress instantly.
  • Via Email: If you cannot access the game, you can request account deletion by contacting us at pumpastudio@gmail.com. We will process your request and delete all data within 48 hours.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: pumpastudio@gmail.com
